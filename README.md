# Data Science Portfolio

A collection of data analysis and machine learning projects completed as part of my 
MIT Data Science and Machine Learning program. Each project works with a real-world 
dataset to extract insights, build predictive models, and reach actionable conclusions.

Built with Python, Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, Matplotlib, and Seaborn.

---

## Projects

### 🏃 Fitness Market Research
Analysis for a treadmill company to understand the profile of customers who purchase 
each product line. Explores demographic and behavioral patterns to support 
data-driven marketing and product decisions.

### 🏠 Melbourne Real Estate Analysis
Exploratory analysis of the Melbourne housing market aimed at providing actionable 
insights for buyers and sellers. Examines pricing trends, location factors, and 
property characteristics.

### 🚗 Uber Demand Analysis
Market research study on Uber's demand profile, identifying patterns in ride requests 
across time, location, and context to better understand supply and demand dynamics.

### 💉 Vaccine Batch Quality Analysis
Quality control analysis of vaccine production batches. Applies statistical and 
machine learning techniques to identify factors that influence batch quality outcomes.
